Premium Stainless Steel Mopping Sink

Engineered for the demands of commercial and industrial cleaning stations, the ADSD stainless steel mopping sink combines rugged durability with effortless hygiene. Fabricated entirely in-house from premium-grade stainless steel, each unit is precision-welded to resist corrosion, impact and the constant exposure to water and cleaning chemicals that defines janitorial environments. The deep, smoothly finished basin allows buckets to be filled and emptied with ease, while integrated mounting points and a floor-level design keep the workspace organised and safe. Ideal for facilities, warehouses, hospitals, hotels and food-processing plants, our mopping sinks are built to withstand years of heavy daily use without staining, warping or losing their finish. Because every sink is manufactured by our own certified fabrication team — never sub-contracted — we guarantee consistent quality, exact specifications and reliable delivery across the UAE. Customers choose ADSD for the strength of the weld, the cleanliness of the finish, and the confidence that comes from dealing directly with the people who build the product.
